Welcome to The Duck Game: Side Quests

These side quests are an extension of The Duck Game designed to lead you through a series of puzzles that will (hopefully) be fun and maybe even boost your NFC knowledge. They are meant to be solved in order so that solving each puzzle will lead you to your next clue.

All answers to any of the ciphers/codes/hints will either give you either a URL that will lead you to a file in the duckducks.org/sidequests directory or give you a word or phrase that you use to access a file in the duckducks.org/sidequests directory. 

For example, if you decode a puzzle and it gives you the answer "quack.txt", the next step in the side quests for you is duckducks.org/sidequests/quack.txt

Enjoy!
