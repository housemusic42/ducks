The Duck Game Instructions

Welcome to The Duck Game! It's a simple game to encourage interaction between humans at the conference using NFC enabled stickers. Each round of the game selects a random winner from those that have joined the round.

The game uses the unique url encoded on each NFC sticker to join a round of the game. Once enough humans join a round, a random winner will be selected for some swag.

How to play:

  • Find yourself a duck sticker. Several humans will be handing them out.
  • Make your sticker visible so other people can see you are playing the game.
  • Scan your sticker so the url opens and joins in for the current round.
  • Watch duckducks.org to see the round winners. There will be multiple rounds per day, so keep scanning throughout the day.
  • The winners are chosen at random from all the humans in the given round, so no additional effort is needed.
  • If you win, come to the meetup location to get your swag (you must have your sticker with you).
  • Keep or dispose of your sticker properly… do not leave it at the conference unless it’s on an approved sticker board.

Talk to others about the stickers and the game. Use them as a conversation starter in line. Have fun.

FAQ

  1. What is The Duck Game?

    The Duck Game is a simple game to encourage interaction between humans at the conference using NFC enabled stickers.

  2. How do I scan my sticker?
    • Android: To enable NFC on your phone: Open settings, find "Connections" or "Connected devices", and then toggle NFC on. Hold the sticker to the back of your phone.
    • iOS: On modern iPhones, NFC should be on. On older iPhones, you may need to open the Control Center and tap the NFC Tag Reader icon.
  3. Does the sticker track me?

    No, the sticker just stores the unique url that allows you to participate in the game.

  4. Do I have to do anything other than scan to play?

    Technically, no, but we encourage you to use the sticker to interact with others.
